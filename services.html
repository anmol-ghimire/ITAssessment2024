About Our Services

Model United Nations (MUN) is an educational activity that stimulates the proceedings of the United Nations, allowing participants to step into the roles of diplomats and representatives of various countries.

During this conference, students engage in debates, negotiations, and problem-solving within assigned committees, addressing global issues such as diplomacy, security, economics, and social affairs.
